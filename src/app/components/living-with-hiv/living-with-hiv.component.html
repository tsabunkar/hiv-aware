04 — Living With HIV

A full life
is possible

With modern antiretroviral therapy (ART), people with HIV live as long as people without HIV. Treatment has transformed HIV from a terminal illness into a manageable chronic condition — like diabetes or high blood pressure.

The most important fact

U = U

Undetectable = Untransmittable

When a person with HIV takes ART consistently and their viral load becomes undetectable, they cannot sexually transmit HIV to their partners. This is one of the most empowering scientific findings in HIV research, confirmed by multiple major studies including PARTNER and HPTN 052.

Antiretroviral Therapy (ART) — How It Works

One pill once a day

Most modern HIV regimens are single-tablet combinations — one pill, once a day. Side effects are much milder than older treatments. Your doctor will find the right regimen for you.
